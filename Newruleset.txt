Here is the bulleted reference sheet for the complete withdrawal and rebalancing ruleset:

Initial State & Targets
Core Portfolio: $700,000 baseline.

Target Allocation: 50% Growth (FBCG/AVUV) / 50% Fixed Income (PYLD/JPIE).

Crisis Buffer: $72,000 (SGOV), held alongside but outside the core.

Initial Withdrawal: $5,000/month (subject to inflation adjustments).

1. Withdrawal Hierarchy (Executed Monthly)
Primary (Normal Operations): Withdrawals are pulled proportionally from the Fixed Income bucket.

Crisis Mode (200-day SMA Tripped): Withdrawals are pulled from the SHY buffer to protect core assets.

Fallback 1 (SGOV Depleted): Withdrawals revert to Fixed Income.

Fallback 2 (Last Resort): Withdrawals are pulled from the Growth bucket only if SHY and Fixed Income are entirely exhausted.

2. Crisis Management (Evaluated Weekly via Growth synthetic price index 200-day SMA)
Halt Rebalance: If the Core Portfolio falls 5% below the 200-day SMA, all standard 5/25 rebalancing is frozen.

Enter Crisis Mode: If the Core Portfolio falls 7.5% below the 200-day SMA, the monthly withdrawal sequence shifts to the SHY buffer.

Exit Crisis Mode: The system exits Crisis Mode only when the Core Portfolio recovers to 3% above the original transition price. Once exited, standard Fixed Income withdrawals resume, and dividends begin slowly refilling the SHY buffer.

3. Annual Adjustments (Evaluated Once a Year in November)
Sequence of Operations: 1. Check Inflation Freeze.
2. Check Special Dividend.
3. Process standard rebalance (if not halted by 200-day SMA).

The Inflation Freeze (12-month SMA): Check the Core Portfolio against a 12-month (52-week) SMA. If the core is down 5% or more, skip the annual standard inflation increase to the monthly withdrawal amount.

The Special Dividend (YoY Growth Return): Check the 1-year return of the Growth bucket (FBCG/AVUV) against the previous November.

Trigger: Growth bucket YoY return is > 25%.

Action: Extract 20% of the excess gains (the profit above the 25% threshold) as a one-time cash bonus, leaving the remainder to be swept into Fixed Income via standard rebalancing.

4. Standard Rebalancing (Evaluated Weekly)
Prerequisite: Must not be halted by the 200-day SMA circuit breaker.

Trigger: Evaluates the 50/50 Growth vs. Fixed Income split. Triggers if an asset class drifts beyond a 5% absolute boundary or a 25% relative boundary.

Action: Sweeps excess capital to restore the 50/50 balance (typically capturing Growth profits to continuously rebuild the Fixed Income runway).

The Cash Raising Execution Logic
1. The Trigger & Target

When to run: The script should execute this function a few days prior to the web-portal's scheduled ACH pull. This guarantees the trades have cleared the T+1 settlement period and the cash is fully settled in the account.

The Target Amount: The baseline target is $5,000 (adjusted for the standard annual inflation). In November, the target becomes the baseline plus the calculated Special Dividend bonus.

2. The Sourcing Hierarchy (Where to Pull From)
The program will evaluate the current market state (via the strategy.py circuit breakers) and the current account balances to route the SELL orders.

Path A: Crisis Mode (200-day SMA tripped)

Action: Sell the target dollar amount from the SHY position.

Fallback: If the SGOV balance is less than the target amount, sell all remaining SHY, and sell the difference proportionally from the Fixed Income bucket (PYLD/JPIE).

Path B: Normal Operations (SMA healthy)

Action: Sell the target dollar amount proportionally from the Fixed Income bucket (PIMIX/JPIE).

Fallback: If the Fixed Income bucket is somehow fully depleted (a catastrophic scenario), pull the remainder from the Growth bucket (FBCG/AVUV) as an absolute last resort.

3. The Order Execution

Calculate the exact dollar amount needed from each specific ticker based on the active Path.

Submit a SELL order using the CashQty parameter (fractional shares based on dollar amount).

Wait for the OrderStatus to confirm Filled.

Log the exact cash amount raised and the source tickers.
